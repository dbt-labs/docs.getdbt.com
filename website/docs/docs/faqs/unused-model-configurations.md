---
title: I got an "unused model configurations" error message, what does this mean?
---
You might have forgotten to nest your configurations under your project name, or you might be trying to apply configurations to a directory that doesn't exist. Check out this [article](https://discourse.getdbt.com/t/faq-i-got-an-unused-model-configurations-error-message-what-does-this-mean/112) to understand more.
